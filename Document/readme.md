This directory serves as the central hub for the formal documentation of the "iRevolution: Apple's iPhone Impact in India" project.

It contains all supplementary theoretical background, research notes, and detailed analytical findings derived from the Tableau data exploration that support the visualizations.

## Documentation Overview

The resources contained within this directory may include but are not limited to:

- **Data Sourcing and Methodology:** Extensive notes detailing where the datasets analyzing iPhone sales, pricing variations, consumer demographics, and market penetration within India were aggregated from, alongside how the data was cleaned and pre-processed before entering Tableau.
- **Narrative Outlines:** Drafts and structural guides for building the chronological data journey presented within the Tableau Story.
- **Key Findings & Insights:** Summaries of the most critical trends and observations uncovered through the dashboard's interactive metrics, such as the relationship between price drops, battery advancements, and adoption spikes in tier-1 Indian cities.
- **Design Decisions:** Explanations for the minimalist, Apple-inspired aesthetic choices selected for both the Tableau embedding structure and the Flask application interface.
